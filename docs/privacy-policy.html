Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

Chad Newbry, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates VaxNear (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Vaccination Records: Names, dates, vaccine types, and provider details you enter manually or scan via QR code.
  • Family Profiles: Names and dates of birth for family members you add.
  • Travel Plans: Destinations and dates for travel-related vaccine recommendations.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your approximate location solely to find nearby vaccination sites. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • HealthKit Data: With your explicit permission, the App reads and writes immunization records to Apple HealthKit. This data remains on your device and in your iCloud Health data; we never receive or store it on our servers.
  • Camera: Used only to scan QR codes on vaccination cards. Images are processed on-device and are not stored or transmitted.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display and manage your vaccination history
  • Provide CDC-based schedule recommendations
  • Locate nearby vaccination providers
  • Sync your data across your devices via iCloud (if enabled)

3. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device and, if you enable iCloud Sync, in your private iCloud container. We do not operate backend servers that receive your health data. The App supports Face ID / device passcode protection for an additional layer of security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data never leaves your device or iCloud container except when you explicitly choose to export it.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple's MapKit to display vaccination site locations. Apple's privacy policy governs data processed by MapKit. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

VaxNear may store vaccination records for children as part of family profiles. This data is entered and controlled solely by the parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Because your data is stored on-device and in your personal iCloud, you have full control. You can delete any records within the App or delete the App entirely to remove all local data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
