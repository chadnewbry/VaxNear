Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data stored?

All your vaccination records are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is also stored in your private iCloud container. We do not have access to your data.

Can I share my records with my doctor?

Yes — use the Export Records feature in Settings to generate a shareable summary of your vaccination history.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual records within the App, or delete the App entirely to remove all local data. If iCloud Sync is enabled, manage your iCloud data through Settings > Apple ID > iCloud on your device.

Is the vaccine schedule data accurate?

VaxNear uses publicly available CDC immunization schedules. The data is for informational purposes only — always consult your healthcare provider for personalized medical advice.

Does VaxNear work offline?

Yes. Your vaccination records and CDC schedule data are available offline. Finding nearby vaccination sites requires an internet connection.
